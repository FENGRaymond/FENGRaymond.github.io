Research

My research interests focus on computer vision, computational imaging and optimization. I like to think of how networks could learn effectively from limited visual data and how to achieve "learning to learn". As for imaging side, I'm interested in projects about inferring physical properties from raw images and incoporating physics into deep learning. BTW, I'm also interested in theoretical study of deep learning though I haven't had a chance to do a relevant project.
